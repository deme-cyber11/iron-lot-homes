# Iron Lot Homes - Keyword Strategy & SEO Roadmap

## The Challenge

Mobile home investing is a low-search-volume niche. Individual keywords like "sell my mobile home Austin" may only get 50-100 searches/month. But the flip side: competition is almost nonexistent. The top organic results are poorly designed, have weak content, and no one is targeting Spanish-speaking searchers.

## The Solution: Volume Through Coverage

Instead of chasing 1-2 high-volume keywords, we build a wide net of hyper-targeted pages that each capture small pockets of traffic. Combined, they generate significant lead flow.

## On-Page SEO Checklist (Every Page)

- [ ] Unique title tag (under 60 chars) with primary keyword
- [ ] Meta description (under 160 chars) with CTA
- [ ] H1 with primary keyword
- [ ] H2s with secondary keywords
- [ ] Keyword in first 100 words
- [ ] Internal links to other service pages
- [ ] Lead capture form
- [ ] Image with keyword-rich alt text
- [ ] LocalBusiness schema markup
- [ ] Mobile responsive
- [ ] Fast load time (under 3 seconds)
