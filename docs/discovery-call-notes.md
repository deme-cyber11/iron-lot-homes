## Website Requirements

### Structure (4 Tabs/Pages)

2. **About Us**

   - Trust builders: years in business, track record, team network

3. **Sellers Page** ("Looking to sell your mobile home?")
   - Lead capture form with specific fields (see below)
   - Simple, direct, minimal barriers

4. **Buyers Page** ("Looking to buy a mobile home?")
   - Lead capture form with buyer-specific fields (see below)
   - Info about owner financing options

5. **Inventory Page** (future/optional)
   - Display available homes with photos + basic info
   - Possibly integrate Zillow/Marketplace listings (needs research)
   - Even simple: uploaded photos + general info

### Seller Lead Form Fields
- Name / Contact info
- Single wide or double wide?
- Year of home
- Bedrooms
- Bathrooms
- Square footage
- Condition of home
- Current mortgage or lien? (Y/N)
- Outstanding balances? How much?
- Location
- When do they want to sell by?

### Buyer Lead Form Fields
- Name / Contact info
- Single wide or double wide preference?
- Bedrooms desired
- Bathrooms desired
- Price range
- Looking for: Major repairs / Minor repairs / Move-in ready?
- Cash purchase or owner financing?
- Timeline to move in
- How much can they afford per month?

### Design Requirements
- **Super simple and user friendly** - "abuela can use it"
- Minimal scrolling to reach CTA
- Form submissions go to business email
- **Bilingual: English + Spanish** (toggle/button switch)
- Mobile-friendly (critical for this demographic)
- Trust-building elements (testimonials later, after Google Business Profile)
