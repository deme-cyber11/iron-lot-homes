Arlington's central location between Dallas and Fort Worth makes it uniquely attractive for mobile home owners. This has helped preserve the character of many of Arlington's residential neighborhoods, including its mobile home communities, which continue to offer affordable housing close to major employment centers. The combination of affordability and location makes Arlington mobile homes consistently valuable.
