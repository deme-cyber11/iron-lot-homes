Bastrop's Mobile Home Parks and Communities

Bastrop County is home to a diverse range of mobile home parks and manufactured housing communities. Tahitian Village, one of the most well-known communities in the area, is a large residential subdivision southeast of downtown Bastrop that features hundreds of mobile and manufactured homes on spacious lots along the Colorado River. Pine Forest is another established community offering residents a quiet, wooded setting with easy access to Highway 71 and the city center.

Heritage Oaks provides affordable manufactured housing in a family-friendly environment, while Colony Park offers residents convenient proximity to Bastrop's downtown amenities and the Colorado River corridor. Beyond these named communities, there are numerous smaller parks and individual mobile homes on private land scattered throughout the Bastrop area, along Cedar Creek Road, Highway 21, and throughout the eastern reaches of the county toward Paige and McDade.
