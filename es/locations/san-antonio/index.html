San Antonio's Mobile Home Parks and Communities

San Antonio and Bexar County are home to hundreds of mobile home parks and manufactured housing communities, making it one of the largest markets for manufactured housing in all of Texas. The Kirby area on the east side of the city features numerous well-established parks that have served military families and working-class residents for decades, benefiting from proximity to Randolph Air Force Base. Von Ormy, located along the I-35 corridor southwest of downtown, has a significant concentration of mobile home communities on both park lots and private acreage.

The South Side of San Antonio, stretching along South Presa Street, Roosevelt Avenue, and the I-37 corridor, is home to some of the city's densest manufactured housing neighborhoods. The West Side communities along Highway 90 and the Medina River corridor, as well as the East Side parks near Converse and along FM 78, round out a vast network of manufactured housing throughout the metro area.
